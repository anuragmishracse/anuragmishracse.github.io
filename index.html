Inline Arduino Skating

Recently I bought my new pair of inline skates. And I have modified it a bit using Arduino, DHT11, JHD 162A, HC SR04 etc.
I am trying to measure the rpm, in turn, the linear velocity of my wheels while I am skating. I have used an ultrasonic sensor, with the help of which I am measuring the speed of my skates only when they are on the ground(about 15 cm). I am also measuring the temperature and humidity of the surrounding. All the values are displayed on the 16*2 LCD display.
I have uploaded its video on YouTube.See the videos below

ATM Emulator with Java

The aim of this project was to make a complete working real-time ATM Emulator. ATM(Automatic Teller Machine) helps people with their transactions and balance, provided they have an account with any bank. Similarly, the bank-manager(consider admin) makes account of people and adds their initial money to their account. The admin has a secure login. Now the users(people) can withraw money. Allowed only if the ATM has that much money. The notes they would get, depends on the availability. Like if there are no Rs 500 notes, then he will get 5*100 notes. The notes are given in priority as 1000>500>100>50. All the conditions are checked.
The database of users and admin is stored in MySQL. Things can be modified only by the admin.
I have uploaded the source code on GitHub.
